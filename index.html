About Me

De Anna Ienopoli is an aspiring professional makeup artist in Toronto, Ontario. Trained at the College of Makeup Art and Design in Fashion, Theater, Film, Special FX and Creature design, she has already worked on several shorts and a feature establishing her hard working nature and eagerness to learn and grow as an artist. Her artistry is not limited solely to makeup, but she is also an accomplished Graphic Designer trained in Adobe Software and is currently learning Zbrush in the interest of utilizing its digital sculpting for concept work related to her field of interest. While she is capable in all ranges, her passion lies in Film makeup and Special FX prosthetics. Her ultimate career goal is to find a permanent home at an FX Studio specializing in prosthetics and creature design. She takes inspiration from such historically great artists as Rick Baker, Stan Winston and Tom Savini and dreams to live up to their legacies in her own small way.
